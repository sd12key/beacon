Shining a Light on Exceptional Flavors

Welcome to The Beacon, premier destination for delicious cuisine and cozy ambiance near the iconic lighthouse. Nestled in the heart of this historic area, The Beacon offers a warm, inviting space where locals and visitors alike can unwind, savor a meal, and enjoy the charm of the waterfront.

Our passion for exceptional food and an atmosphere that reflects our unique surroundings is evident in every dish and every corner of our establishment. Our menu celebrates a variety of flavors, featuring local ingredients and global inspirations. From fresh seafood to comforting classics, we offer an experience designed to be both memorable and nourishing. Whether it’s brunch by the sea, a family dinner, or a relaxing evening in our lounge, The Beacon is the ideal setting for every occasion. Our chefs are dedicated to creating dishes that not only satisfy your appetite but also capture the spirit of the coast.

We are thrilled to introduce our new website, designed to make your experience with us even more convenient. You can now place orders for pickup directly through our online platform, making it easier than ever to bring The Beacon home.

Simply browse our menu, customize your choices, and swing by to pick up a freshly prepared meal whenever it suits you. We invite you to come experience The Beacon, where each visit is a chance to reconnect with great food, good company, and the vibrant heritage of our town.

We Are In The News!

The Beacon Restaurant
Shines Bright

Nestled near the iconic Beacon Hill lighthouse, The Beacon has quickly become a standout on on out town's dining map. Since its grand opening, this cozy establishment has been serving up exceptional coastal cuisine that has both locals and tourists raving.

“The atmosphere here is simply unique,” shared Jane O’Connell, a long-time resident. “The view of the lighthouse from the dining room creates such a tranquil setting, and the food is simply outstanding!” From their famous Grilled Ribeye Steak to the seasonal seafood specialties, The Beacon’s menu caters to a variety of tastes. Head Chef Liam Donovan, a native of Newfoundland, prides himself on using the freshest local ingredients to craft dishes that capture the flavors of the Atlantic.

For those wanting to experience The Beacon’s charm at home, the restaurant now offers convenient pickup orders through their newly launched website. Whether you’re craving a hearty meal after a day’s hike or celebrating a special occasion, The Beacon promises an unforgettable dining experience.

“It’s not just a restaurant; it’s a place where people come to enjoy Newfoundland’s natural beauty and hospitality,” says owner Emily Walsh. The Beacon truly lives up to its name, shining as a beacon of warmth, flavor, and Newfoundland pride.
